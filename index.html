Authentic Conversations

Whenever you open a video (or "track"), your reaction is recorded as you watch, capturing true moments of laughter, surprise, and meaning that are missing from other messaging apps.

Real Responses

Once your reaction is recorded, you can add more in a response which sends back to your friend. These reaction and response videos create a continuous, real-time conversation.

One Tap Conversations

Just a single tap connects you with anyone in the world. Once you're done, swipe to send your video. Want to send more? Simply tap their name again to add to the conversation.
