Commonly Asked

How do I rewatch a video?

You don’t.

Can I stop Tracks from sending a video after I start reacting?

No.

What if I say something wrong by accident?

Say sorry.

Ok, some more legitimate questions:

How do I know when a video is sent?

Tracks sends videos as soon as you stop recording your response. The indicators to the right of the recipient's name will tell you how long ago the last video was sent.

How do I end a conversation?

Conversations on Tracks never end, they just get put on hold. You can use the indicators to the right of a recipient’s name to let you know how long their message is. If you receive a very short track, you might know the conversations is paused from the context. Pick up where you left off whenever you like by taping their name!

What if I don’t have wifi or a data connection?

If your wifi or data connection is not strong enough to transfer the video, it is stored on your device until you regain connection. Stored videos are sent as soon as connection is restored.

Does Tracks store my videos after I watch them?

No. Your private videos can only be watched once, and are deleted from our servers as soon as they are watched. The only videos we can watch are the ones you send to Team Tracks.

How do I block a user or flag content?

If you wish to report, block or flag objectionable content or users, you can do so in a number of ways. The primary method is to use the contact form in the app. This form includes a category of message which allows you to “Flag or Block Content or Users”. We will act on objectionable content and user reports within 24 hours by removing the content and ejecting the user who provided the offending content.

You may also email legal@getrailroad.com, which is in the terms of service; or you may send a video to us, “Team Tracks” (our open line of communication with users), with comments or reports. Via any of these methods we will act on objectionable content and user reports within 24 hours.
